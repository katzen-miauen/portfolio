Anna Chekushkina is specializing in multimedia art, motion and graphic design. Her practice includes advertising projects, music videos, and interactive design. Her main interests are research, development of systems and tools, and experiments in the field of generative graphics.

FOMOTEKA promo reel

advertising

A promotional video for FOMOTEKA, an internet journal that covers the latest events in Moscow’s cultural scene. The video is used as an introduction before partner film screenings and as a short clip for existing audiences. FOMOTEKA stands out from other media by its authorial approach, where the content is not impersonal and the authors talk about what they are passionate about.

HYPEROBJECTS

book design

The visual research is devoted to the phenomenon of hyperobjects, which are widespread objects in space and time, and how modern art interprets them. The concept was proposed by philosopher Timothy Morton, and includes such global entities as global warming, the biosphere, the Earth, all plastic on the planet, and so on.

Neverbeen

album covers

DIRTY GLASSEZ’s album Neverbeen was conceived as a work that evokes feelings of melancholy and nostalgia for unlived lives. Places that have never been seen, people they did not know, houses they did not live in.

art

POV

speculative design

Group project with Shechkov Stepa

POV is a speculative project that reflects from position of the engineers of the future on how to give a person the opportunity to feel themselves real. Year 2073. The creators tell their story about the process of finding a design invention that will help people adapt to nature after a long stay in virtual reality.

I'm home

VR

Mocumentary project about significant places from the past that you want to return to. This is a series of three 360° videos in which neural networks are used to recreate a full-fledged scene of the entire environment from old photographs.
